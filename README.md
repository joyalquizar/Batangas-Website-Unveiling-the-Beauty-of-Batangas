Batangas Website: Unveiling the Beauty of Batangas is my first web development project, created to showcase the rich culture, history, natural attractions, and tourist destinations of Batangas Province, Philippines.Batangas is one of the most popular tourist destinations near Metro Manila. It is known for the iconic Taal Volcano, historic ancestral houses in Taal, beautiful churches, stunning beaches, and world-class diving spots such as Anilao, Laiya, Punta Fuego, Matabungkay, and Calatagan. The province is also part of the Verde Island Passage, recognized as the center of the world's marine biodiversity. This website serves as an informative tourism platform that highlights the province's attractions, cultural heritage, and natural beauty while providing visitors with an engaging and user-friendly browsing experience.
